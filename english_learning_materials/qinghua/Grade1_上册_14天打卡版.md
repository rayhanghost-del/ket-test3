## Day 8: 综合复习（一）

### 复习内容
- 身体部位：eye, ear, mouth
- 动词：have, is, can, like
- 颜色：red, blue, green, yellow, black

### 练习题

2. This ______ my eye. (have/is)
3. I ______ red. (like/have)

### 对话2：谈论喜好
