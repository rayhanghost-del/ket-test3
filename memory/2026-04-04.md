### Key Insights - SKU Substitution Analysis

1. **Tarot Cards: Multi-SKU strategy required**
   - Single SKU stockout (e.g., 黑金 in March) has partial substitution effect
   - However, March data shows **all SKUs declined simultaneously** (棱镜:17→1, 绿月:23→2, 蓝月:13→1)
   - **Conclusion**: When 3+ SKUs stock out simultaneously, total sales collapse (70→53→4→0)
   - **Strategy**: Must maintain inventory across ALL 4 SKUs, not just best-sellers

2. **Yoga Mats: Clear hierarchy with limited substitution**
   - Pink (粉) = Core SKU: 64% of sales, 3.2/day average
   - Purple (紫) = Secondary: 22% of sales
   - When purple stockout (Feb 11-20): Pink increased 17 units but purple lost 25
   - **Substitution rate**: ~70% (pink captured most of purple's demand)
   - **Conclusion**: Pink can partially cover purple stockout, but pink itself cannot stockout

3. **March Sales Reality Check**
   - Tarot: 53件 in early March, 4件 mid-March, 0件 late March (gradual decline, not sudden zero)
   - Yoga Pink: 53件 early, 30件 mid, 0件 late (similar pattern)
   - Indicates **staggered stockouts**, not simultaneous
