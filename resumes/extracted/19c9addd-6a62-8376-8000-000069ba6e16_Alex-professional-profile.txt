Hi, I’m Alex! Passionate PE Teacher and Coach from Serbia, now dedicated to
inspiring students in China through engaging physical education. With deep
commitment, I encourage students to develop physical strength, teamwork, and
confidence, making sports an exciting and rewarding part of their daily lives.

My classes are all about energy, creativity, and teamwork. I mix traditional games
with sports, making sure every lesson is inclusive and engaging.
Every student matters in my gym - I encourage them to challenge themselves,
support each other, and celebrate their progress. Seeing kids grow stronger,
happier, and more confident through physical activity is what makes teaching PE
truly rewarding for me.
