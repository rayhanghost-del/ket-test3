# JD: English Homeroom Teacher in Futian, Shenzhen

## Position
English Homeroom Teacher (幼儿园/小学班主任)

## Location
Futian District, Shenzhen

## Requirements
- Native English Speaker
- Bachelor degree or above, relevant educational qualification certificate is preferred
- At least one year teaching experience with young kids from 2-6 years old
- Enjoy staying with kids
- Energetic, patient, responsible
- Excellent interpersonal & communication skill

## Job Duties
1. Demonstrate an understanding of curriculum, subject content, pedagogical knowledge, and the needs of students by providing relevant learning experiences
2. Design coherent instruction and engage students in learning
3. Manage classroom procedures and managing student behaviors
4. Create an environment of respect and support
5. Equipment set up and maintenance, such as teaching materials and classroom decoration
6. Document students' learning and assess students to improve teaching and learning
7. Participate in school-wide teaching activities
8. A reliable resource for parents

## Benefits
- **Salary**: 25-28K after tax
- **Housing allowance**: 4,000 RMB/month
- **Yearly Contract Bonus**: Foreign employees entitled to contract bonus per academic year
- **Flight allowance**: Reimbursement up to RMB 8,000 before tax per academic year (economy class)
- **Relocation allowance**: Hotel allowance provided based on home base/last posting location
- **Medical Insurance**: Social and medical insurance included
- **Education for Teachers' Children**: First child gets 100% tuition remission
- **Meal Allowance**: Breakfast, lunch, snacks provided
- **Health Examination**: Once per academic year
- **Paid Leave**: School winter/summer vacation per school calendar
- **Training & Professional Development**: Reimbursement up to RMB 6,000 for learning programs

## Key Matching Criteria for Candidates
- **Must**: Native English speaker
- **Must**: Experience with young children (2-6 years old)
- **Preferred**: Bachelor+ with education qualification
- **Preferred**: Homeroom/early childhood experience
